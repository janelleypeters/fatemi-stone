Service 03

Stone Protection

Natural stone rewards a little care. Follow these fundamentals — or ask us for a product plan matched to your exact material.

01

Seal

Most porous stones — marble, limestone, travertine — benefit from a penetrating sealer that resists staining without changing the look.

  • Test absorption with the water-drop test
  • Re-seal high-use surfaces every 1–3 years
  • Granite & quartzite need less frequent sealing
02

Clean

Use pH-neutral stone cleaners only. Acids in vinegar, citrus, and many household sprays etch calcium-based stone permanently.

  • Blot spills immediately — don’t wipe
  • Skip vinegar, bleach & abrasive pads
  • Use coasters under wine, oil & citrus
03

Maintain

Small habits keep stone looking new. For etches or dull spots, professional honing and polishing restores the original finish.

  • Dust and damp-mop floors regularly
  • Use felt pads under furniture & decor
  • Schedule periodic professional refinishing
